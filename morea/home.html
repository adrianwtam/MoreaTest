Welcome to MATH 483, Spring 2023

MATH 483 is a 60 hour course on advanced calculus and quantum differential equations.

Who should take this course

This course is intended for undergraduates in computer science and mathematics to develop skills in quantum computing as a prerequisite for ICS 632!

Pedagogy

MATH 483 is structured as a sequential series of modules, each taking approximately a week to complete. Each module has the following structure:

  • Prerequisites, describing skills you should have prior to starting the module.
  • Learning outcomes, describing the major goals for the module.
  • Readings and other online resources, providing background material.
  • Experiential learning activities, providing a structured set of challenges enabling you to acquire mastery of material.
  • Assessments, which help you determine if you have acquired mastery of the material.
